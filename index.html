Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lumo Entertainment has been bringing joy and unforgettable moments to Melbourne for over a decade. We specialize in creating magical experiences through our diverse range of entertainment services - from intimate birthday parties to grand corporate events.

Our team of professional entertainers, event coordinators, and technical experts work together to ensure every event is perfectly tailored to your needs. We're not just service providers; we're memory makers who understand that every celebration is unique.

Why choose us? Because we believe in excellence, creativity, and making your vision come to life. With our extensive network of talented performers and state of the art equipment, we're the premier choice for entertainment in the industry.
